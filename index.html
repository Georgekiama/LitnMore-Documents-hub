How checklists work
When a new Deal is created in HubSpot, the Onboarding checklist opens automatically in ClickUp. When a deal moves to Pre-Delivery, the QC checklist opens. Check off each item as you complete it. Both are required — nothing ships without a completed QC checklist.
Reminder
Per MSA §4.6 — nothing is released to a client until all outstanding amounts are paid. Confirm payment cleared before the QC sign-off.
